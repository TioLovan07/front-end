About Us

History of Our Company

Coffee is a popular drink in the world, including in Indonesia. In Bali, coffee has become part of the culture and life of the people. We, Parta Kopi Bali, have been established since 1980, we produce and market local coffee. And recently we changed the logo and company name to Parta Coffee.

This company was founded by two brothers, namely Ketut Parama and his brother Made Arta. They are both coffee farmers who only sell raw natural products without any other processing. Over time, for 15 years only selling raw natural products, finally this business began to develop and started producing ground coffee.

Parta Coffee using Arabica coffee beans that come from coffee plantations that are grown and maintained by ourselves. These coffee beans are processed traditionally using simple machines. Coffee from Parta Coffee has a distinctive taste, namely a strong and bitter taste.
