The Type of Coffee Machine We Use

Feby / 22 September 2023

At our company we have several types of coffee machines such as volumetric coffee machines and fully automatic coffee machines. Volumetric coffee machines are automatic coffee devices designed to provide convenience and consistency in brewing coffee. By using volumetric technology, it is possible to measure water and coffee measurements accurately, resulting in consistent coffee brewing.

The Nuova Simonelli Aurellia Wave is a professional espresso coffee machine that is user friendly, making the coffee making process more practical. With T3 Technology, this coffee machine can help baristas to regulate the temperature easily. Apart from that, the Nuova Simonelli Aurellia Wave also has pulse jet technology, which can optimize water flow and pressure during the discharge phase. With these various settings, the barista can control all aspects of making espresso coffee.

Allegra Luce 1 Group, this series of coffee machines can be considered a miracle in the world of coffee lovers. Created by Allegra, this coffee machine combines elegant design with powerful functionality. The boiler capacity is 3L and is supported by 2000 watts of power, ensuring a fast and consistent brewing process. Its dimensions are 41 cm wide, 43 cm deep and 55 cm high, making it a compact but powerful machine, suitable for various spaces.Weighing 34 kg, the Allegra Luce remains stable during operation, this coffee machine operates on a voltage of 220/240V, providing power required for a smooth coffee brewing experience.

And there is also a fully automatic coffee machine. This machine is a coffee device designed to simplify the coffee brewing process to the maximum. These machines combine a grinder, brewer, and often a water storage system in one unit.

Microbar II, is a machine designed for a capacity of up to 100 espressos per day, with dimensions of 48 cm high, 45 cm deep and 32 cm wide. This machine weighs 33 kg making it stable, while the 5 lt water tank ensures sufficient water supply . The grinding system with a choice of 1 or 2 50 mm grinders allows for a variety of coffee flavors. The user interface is equipped with a 2-line LCD screen, providing easy control. The 500 gr coffee bean hopper and stainless steel brewing chamber with diecast aluminum add reliability and durability.
